# Site Inspection Report
> Property: Maple Court Commercial Unit 4
> Date: 2026-07-08
> Inspector: R. Menon (Field Engineer)

## Area: Main Electrical Room
Visual inspection of the main distribution panel showed one breaker with
discolouration and a faint burning odour. The panel surface was warm to the
touch near the top-left breaker. No active sparking was observed at the time
of visit.

## Area: First Floor Ceiling (Above Reception)
A large brown water stain was noted on the ceiling with bubbling and peeling
paint around the recessed light fitting. The substrate felt damp to touch.
![Water stain and bubbling paint on first-floor ceiling](images/inspection_ceiling.png)

## Area: Ground Floor East Wall (Office)
A diagonal crack was observed running from the window corner toward the floor,
approximately 300 mm in length. Minor paint separation along the crack line.
![Diagonal crack at ground-floor east wall window](images/inspection_wall.png)

## Area: Roof Membrane (Plant Room)
Walkthrough inspection of the roof membrane and plant-room envelope found no
defect observed on the roof surface. No visible ponding or penetration noted.
