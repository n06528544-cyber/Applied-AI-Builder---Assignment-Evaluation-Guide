# Thermal Imaging Report
> Property: Maple Court Commercial Unit 4
> Date: 2026-07-08
> Operator: Thermal Survey (FLIR E8)

## Area: Main Electrical Room
Thermal scan of the distribution panel identified a concentrated hotspot at the
top-left breaker reading 71°C against a reference temperature of 28°C. The
surrounding breakers measured within normal range (30-34°C).
![Thermal hotspot at main panel breaker](images/thermal_panel.png)

## Area: Roof / Plant Room
Thermal image of the roof deck shows a distinct cooler patch measuring
approximately 19°C compared with an ambient roof temperature of 27°C. The
temperature depression is consistent with elevated moisture content / wet
insulation in that zone.
![Cool moisture patch on roof deck thermal image](images/thermal_roof.png)

## Area: First Floor Ceiling (Above Reception)
No dedicated thermal scan was performed for this area during the survey.
